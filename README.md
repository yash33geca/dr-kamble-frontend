Dr. Nishant Kamble is a highly qualified Rheumatologist and Clinical Immunologist specializing in the diagnosis and treatment of autoimmune, inflammatory, and musculoskeletal diseases. He completed his MBBS from Government Medical College, Nagpur, MD in General Medicine from Dr. S.N. Medical College, Jodhpur, and DM in Clinical Immunology and Rheumatology from the prestigious King George's Medical University (KGMU), Lucknow.

He has also earned the Specialty Certificate Examination (SCE) in Rheumatology from the Royal College of Physicians, United Kingdom, and is EULAR (European Alliance of Associations for Rheumatology) Certified.

Dr. Kamble has served as Senior Resident and Assistant Professor at Government Medical College, Nagpur, and as Assistant Professor in the Department of Clinical Immunology and Rheumatology, combining patient care with teaching and academic research.

He has expertise in treating Rheumatoid Arthritis, Ankylosing Spondylitis, Psoriatic Arthritis, Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE), Sjögren's Syndrome, Vasculitis, Myositis, Systemic Sclerosis, Osteoporosis, Gout, and other autoimmune and connective tissue disorders. His practice focuses on accurate diagnosis, evidence-based treatment, patient education, and long-term disease management.

Known for his compassionate and patient-centered approach, Dr. Kamble currently serves as a Visiting Consultant Rheumatologist at KIMS-Kingsway Hospital, KRIMS Hospitals, Shankara Superspeciality Hospital, and Taywade Hospital, Nagpur, providing advanced rheumatology care across Central India.
